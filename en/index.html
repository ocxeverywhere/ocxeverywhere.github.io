Developer Notes

Development principles

Rather than adding features for their own sake, I prioritize flows that users can understand immediately and complete with confidence. Even a small app should treat data handling and privacy guidance as part of the product.

01

Reduce repetitive work

Use thoughtful automation to lighten routines such as reading the same book every night or watching a long video from start to finish.

02

Respect the on-device experience

Keep data on the device whenever possible, and explain clearly when a feature requires external processing.

03

Ship small and keep improving

Put useful apps in real users' hands first, then refine each feature as its practical use becomes clearer.
