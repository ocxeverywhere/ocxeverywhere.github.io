# Website Privacy

This page describes the LazyDaddy website at `ocxeverywhere.github.io`. Individual apps may process information differently; consult the privacy policy on the relevant app website for product-specific details.

## Website hosting

This site is published through GitHub Pages. Requests to view the site are handled by GitHub's infrastructure and are subject to GitHub's applicable privacy information.

## Analytics

The site supports Google Analytics configuration. When no measurement ID is configured, the site's Google Analytics integration is disabled. If analytics is enabled, the site will load Google Analytics to measure visits and interactions.

## External links

The site links to app stores, app websites, GitHub, and Ko-fi. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

## Email

If you contact LazyDaddy by email, the information you provide is used to read and respond to your message. Do not send passwords, payment details, or other sensitive information.

## Questions

For privacy questions about this website, email [ocxeverywhere@gmail.com](mailto:ocxeverywhere@gmail.com).
