## 💡 Why a Parenting Automation App Is So Helpful!

### Building a Healthy Habit of Turning Pages Independently

What I liked was that the child wasn’t just staring at a screen—the book stayed in his hands the whole time.

As he turned the pages, he would ask, “What does it say here?” and request to hear his favorite scenes again.

Listening and turning the pages at the right moment can support the development of fine motor skills while also helping children build an independent, self-directed reading habit.

### Protecting Mom and Dad’s Voices & Making Time to Rest

Reading Korean storybooks aloud can sometimes feel like a daily assignment, but doing it this way made the burden feel a little lighter.

Some days, Dad reads the entire book in his own voice. On days like today, the app lends a hand instead... ☕
