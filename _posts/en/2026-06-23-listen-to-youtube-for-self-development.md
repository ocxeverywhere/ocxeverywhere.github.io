## A New Way to Practice Digital Detox by Listening

YouTube is overflowing with genuinely valuable information these days.

Economics, self-development, English, history, science, IT, investing, and more.

Every day, new videos are uploaded that can teach us a great deal from just one episode.

But there is one problem.

**We pick up our phones to open YouTube, only to realize that we are watching Shorts.**

As we scroll through recommended videos, we often lose track of the content we originally intended to watch.

---

## What If You Want the Information but Not More Screen Time?

Think about how often we find ourselves in situations like these throughout the day.

✔ On the subway during the commute

✔ While driving

✔ While taking a walk

✔ While doing housework

✔ While exercising

In many of these moments, what we actually need is not the **"video," but the "information."**

---

YouTube is fundamentally designed to keep us looking at a screen.

We open it to consume useful content, but countless recommendations and Shorts often take up our time instead.

This is also one reason why interest in **digital detox** has been growing.

Research and real-world examples continue to show that reducing smartphone use and unnecessary screen exposure can help improve focus and productivity.

---

## What If You Could Listen to YouTube Like a Podcast?

That is why I created **JustListen**.

JustListen is an app that retrieves captions from YouTube videos and converts them into natural-sounding speech.

That means:

✅ No need to keep the screen on

✅ No getting pulled into recommended videos

✅ No wasting time on Shorts

✅ Full focus on the content itself

You can listen to the YouTube content you want just as you would listen to an audiobook or podcast.

![Feature illustration showing YouTube content listened to through JustListen without watching the screen](/assets/images/posts/justlisten-youtube-audio-feature-en.png)

## Who Is It For?

### 📚 People Who Regularly Watch Self-Development Content

For videos about economics, investing, productivity, and motivation, the message often matters more than the visuals.

You can learn plenty by listening during your commute.

---

### 🎧 People Learning English

You can repeatedly listen to videos with English captions in audio form.

It can also be used for listening practice.

---

### 🌿 People Who Want a Digital Detox

> "I want to consume valuable content, but I want to spend less time looking at my phone."

JustListen began with exactly this concern.

---

## The Truth Is, I Built the App Because I Needed It

These are the times when I use it most:

✔ While driving

✔ While taking a walk

✔ While grocery shopping

✔ While washing dishes

✔ While putting my child to sleep

✔ While exercising

Audio content feels especially valuable while driving because looking at a screen is not an option.

---

## The Content Matters More Than the Screen

We often think of YouTube as something we "watch."

But in reality, **we use YouTube to get information.**

If that is the case, do we really need to look at the screen?

Sometimes we need to put the phone in our pocket,

turn off the screen,

and take some time to focus entirely through listening.

---

## JustListen Is an App for Those Moments

Keep the valuable content,

reduce unnecessary screen consumption,

and focus entirely on listening.
