## A Little Time for Myself While My Child Sleeps

When I am putting my child to sleep and finally have a little time to myself, I have recently been enjoying a mobile game called **Kingshot**.

![English Kingshot screen from my gaming routine while putting my child to sleep](/assets/images/posts/multitasking-youtube-kingshot-routine-en.png)

## Pairing Daily Missions with YouTube I Wanted to Hear

Daily missions are not always the most exciting part of a game, right? That is when I listen to YouTube content that I had been meaning to watch—or, more accurately, hear.

First, I open a YouTube video that interests me. That puts it in my watch history so I can find it again later. Then I tap **Share** on one of those videos and copy its link.

![English YouTube share menu used to copy a video's link](/assets/images/posts/multitasking-youtube-video-history-en.png)

## Paste It into JustListen, and That Is It

I paste the copied link into **JustListen** and start listening. That is all it takes!

![English JustListen screen for pasting a copied YouTube URL](/assets/images/posts/multitasking-youtube-justlisten-input-en.png)

Now I have a satisfying morning routine: putting my child to sleep, playing a game, and listening to insightful YouTube content at the same time.

![English JustListen playback screen with controls for listening to YouTube content](/assets/images/posts/multitasking-youtube-justlisten-playing-en.png)

If my child will only nap in the stroller, I can even add some light exercise. That is real multitasking, isn't it? Haha.

I also find **Knowledge iN Guest Talk** comfortable to listen to, and I learn a lot and pick up plenty of insights from it. If you know another YouTube channel that works especially well as audio, please recommend it in the comments!
