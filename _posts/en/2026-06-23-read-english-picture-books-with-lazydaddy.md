## “Pick Any Book”—But Why That One?!

It is an ordinary moment at the bookshelf: Dad tells his child, **“Pick any book you like,”** and lets them choose freely. 🥰

But after passing rows of Korean books, the child’s hand stops in the English picture book section!

That instant reaction: “Uh, uh, uh…” 😂

We are not native speakers, and reading an English book aloud can make us tense up and start sweating before we even begin. That is the honest reality for many parents, isn’t it?

Some books come with native-speaker audio, but we tend to look it up only at first. Once the files and links become difficult to manage, it is often impossible to find the right recording when we actually need it.

Fortunately, we have our smartphones for moments like this.
